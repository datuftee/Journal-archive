## Transcript

June

Dear Ann,
Thanks for your letter. Breakfast is working out very well. Got me supplies at Pharmacy, Drug Mart. The chef supplies are the ones Jim's friend used etc. The wonderful Eddie wants and needs - remember suggested alternatives, pictures. The best part - a golf course!

26-Took a long ride- by himself 9:30 played to Washington. Golf of 4 hours. Met 2 men waiting since 4:00. Tried it at Hilton Hills swim - 10 o'clock.
Back 9:30

27

Tunney Duttes

14 Doug would like to go to W'st Coast for a vacation this winter - off with the sunset -
Bernie is busy - so I think Peggy is going to take his Col. Eagle at the Tournament.

5 Phil & Carol might not - working is a pleasant business & that the weather will be as good being as when you camp home.

6 Our caravan was reduced to us the past people work book, as we met many others here on the table of residence - I hope I can walk stop at the Croix Que.

7 Doug said we gave a memo to Rosell Tom or Save Geer. On Dan's comment to me in a camp lesson.
